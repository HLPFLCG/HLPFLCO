About HLPFL

Our Story & Mission

Our Story

HLPFL was founded on the principle of just that - to be helpful. We specialize in assisting the dreamer, visionary, entrepreneur with providing all of the necessary tools to ensure your business has the proper foundation in order to create a thriving and successful business, no strings attached.

Founded by a 24 year old entrepreneur who may not know exactly what to do, but can help others not make the same mistakes he did that led to bankruptcy, business failures and loss of friendships along the way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help first-time entrepreneurs establish their businesses correctly from the start, avoiding the costly mistakes and pitfalls that derail so many promising ventures.

Our Approach

We're not your traditional consulting agency - we focus on the little guy, ensuring your original idea and vision is protected from not only yourself, but those around you seeking to take down what you've worked so hard to build.

We handle everything from legal to web development and everything in between, ensuring you have all the necessary ingredients for your business to be successful in today's volatile economy.
