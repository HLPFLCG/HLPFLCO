Our Journey

From Startup Dreams to Business Reality

Every great business starts with a vision. Our story began when we witnessed too many brilliant entrepreneurs fail—not because their ideas weren't good enough, but because they lacked the legal and branding foundation to succeed.

2019

The Awakening

After seeing countless promising startups crumble under preventable legal issues, our founder realized there was a massive gap in the market. Entrepreneurs needed more than just legal advice—they needed a partner who understood their journey.

2020

HLPFL is Born

HLPFL was officially founded with a simple mission: help first-time founders build legally bulletproof businesses with standout brands. We started with just three clients and a vision to change how entrepreneurs approach business formation.

2021

Rapid Growth

Word spread quickly about our unique approach. We helped over 100 businesses launch successfully, developed our signature methodology, and expanded our team to include specialists in privacy law, brand strategy, and digital marketing.

2022

Innovation & Expansion

We launched our VIP community, created comprehensive legal templates, and developed cutting-edge privacy-first solutions. Our clients began seeing unprecedented success rates, with 98% of HLPFL-launched businesses still thriving after two years.

2023

Industry Recognition

HLPFL became the go-to consultancy for serious entrepreneurs. We were featured in major publications, spoke at industry conferences, and our clients collectively generated over $50 million in protected revenue.

2024

The Future is Now

Today, we're not just a consultancy—we're a movement. With over 500 successful business launches, a thriving community of entrepreneurs, and cutting-edge AI-powered tools, we're redefining what it means to start a business in the modern era.
