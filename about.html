About HLPFL

Our Story & Mission

Our Story

HLPFL was founded on the principle of being helpful to entrepreneurs with big dreams. We specialize in assisting visionaries and dreamers by providing all the necessary tools to ensure your business has the proper foundation for success—no strings attached.

Founded by a 24-year-old entrepreneur who learned valuable lessons through bankruptcy, business failures, and lost friendships, we're committed to helping others avoid these same pitfalls on their entrepreneurial journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help first-time entrepreneurs establish their businesses correctly from the start, avoiding the costly mistakes and pitfalls that derail so many promising ventures. We believe that with the right foundation, any business can thrive.

Our Approach

We're not your traditional consulting agency. We focus on the little guy, ensuring your original idea and vision is protected—not only from external threats but also from common mistakes entrepreneurs make when starting out.

From legal formation to web development and everything in between, we handle all aspects of building your business foundation, ensuring you have all the necessary ingredients for success in today's challenging economy.

Protection First

We prioritize legally protecting your business and personal assets from day one.

Transparency

Clear communication and honest guidance through every step of your business journey.

Innovation

Creative solutions tailored to your unique business vision and goals.
