Our Story

HLPFL Co founded with a simple mission: to help first-time founders navigate the complex world of business formation, legal compliance, and brand development. We believe that great ideas deserve the best foundation to grow and thrive.

After witnessing countless entrepreneurs struggle with the legal and operational aspects of starting a business, we created a comprehensive solution that combines legal expertise, business strategy, and creative branding to provide a holistic approach to business development.

Meet Our Founder

With over a decade of experience in business law and strategy, our founder has helped hundreds of entrepreneurs build legally solid, privacy-first businesses with standout brands.

Combining expertise in legal compliance, business operations, and digital strategy, our founder created HLPFL Consulting to provide comprehensive solutions that address the unique challenges faced by first-time business owners.

Our Approach

At HLPFL Co, we believe in a holistic approach to business development. We don't just help you file paperwork or create a logo—we help you build a solid foundation for long-term success.

Our process begins with understanding your vision and goals, then creating a customized plan that addresses your specific needs and challenges. We work closely with you every step of the way, providing guidance, support, and expertise to help you navigate the complex world of business ownership.
